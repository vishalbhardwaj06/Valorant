Valorant Seasons and Episodes Overview

In Valorant, each season, known as an "Episode" and further divided into "Acts," introduces new content, including agents, maps, skins, and gameplay updates. Episodes typically bring significant changes, such as a new agent or map, while Acts provide ongoing adjustments, new weapon skins, battle passes, and competitive refreshes. These seasonal updates aim to keep the game dynamic and engaging, ensuring fresh experiences for players with each progression.
